Frequently Asked Questions

HyperCharge is Xiaomi's advanced fast-charging technology that can charge your phone up to 120W, allowing you to fully charge your device in just minutes. It includes multiple safety features and temperature control to ensure safe charging.

The 200MP camera uses advanced pixel-binning technology to combine multiple pixels into one larger pixel, resulting in clearer, brighter photos, especially in low light conditions. It can also capture full 200MP resolution photos for incredibly detailed shots.

HyperOS is Xiaomi's latest operating system that replaces MIUI. It offers improved performance, better battery life, seamless connectivity between devices, and a more intuitive user interface designed for modern smartphone users.

Xiaomi phones come with a standard 1-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects. The warranty includes both parts and labor. Extended warranty options are available through our service centers.
